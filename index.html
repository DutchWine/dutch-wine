Bio

Dutch Wine are a hard hitting three piece characterised by atmospheric guitars, emotive vocals and a driving rhythm section. While their sound has been hailed by Tenement TV as “undeniably fresh and contemporary”, the band have been captivating fans across the country with their animated and powerful live shows.

Drawing on inspiration from icons like Pixies, Interpol and DIIV to name but a few, they aim to surprise and excite listeners with each new release. After a barrage of sold out shows, festival appearances and support slots with the likes of Surf Curse, Pom Poko and Last Boy throughout the year, Dutch Wine will headline Stereo on 10th December for their EP launch.
